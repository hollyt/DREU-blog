Week 4 - 06.16.14 - 06.20.14

I ran into many unexpected challenges this week pertaining to the data set I was given to train and test my algorithm with. I finished the implementation of the Friendship Classifier in C++ and started training and testing using leave-one-dyad-out cross validation. After doing multiple iterations of training and testing and getting very low accuracy results, I started to look though the data manually and found that there were many problems with the data set.

Most of this week was taken up by meetings and working with the lab manager to find the correct data set. Hopefully next week I’ll receive the correct data from the dyadic tutoring sessions.
